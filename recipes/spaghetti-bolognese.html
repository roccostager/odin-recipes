Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghettie bolognese is a very popular dish! It's depth depends entirely on the chef!

Ingredients

  • Pasta, of your choice!
  • Tomatoes
  • Carrots
  • Red wine
  • Beef mince!
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Chop onions and carrots into small bits.
  2. Crush garlic.
  3. Add olive oil into pan.
  4. Fry chopped onions and garlic in pan until golden-brown.
  5. Add the beef mince until it is cooked through.
  6. From here, add the tomatoes, red wine, and carrots.
  7. Simmer on medium heat until you have a rich thick sauce.
  8. Boil the pasta in water until al dente!
  9. Slap things onto a plate and devour!
