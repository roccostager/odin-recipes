Tomato Egg Drop Soup

Description

An absolute classic chinese dish! It's super easy to make and always fills you up. You can even add some extra things to your liking!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes!
  • Eggs!
  • Salt
  • Peanut Oil

Steps

  1. Whisk egg within a seperate bowl.
  2. Chop tomatoes into little bits.
  3. Fry tomatoes in a pan with the peanut oil.
  4. Add water to form the soup base.
  5. Add salt into soup until it is to your liking.
  6. Allow the soup to come to a boil, then pour in the whisked egg slowly while stirring quickly.
  7. Once the egg is cooked, the soup is ready to serve.
